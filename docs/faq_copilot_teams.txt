# Copilot FAQ - Copilot and Teams Meetings

Q: What does Copilot do in Teams meetings?
A: Copilot can generate live transcripts, summarize discussion points, capture decisions, and draft follow-up tasks.

Q: Can Copilot identify action items?
A: Yes. Copilot highlights key tasks and decisions from meeting transcripts.

Q: Are meeting transcripts private?
A: Yes. Only meeting participants and authorized members can access transcripts generated by Copilot.

Q: Can I ask Copilot questions after a meeting?
A: Yes. You can ask questions like “What did we decide about project timelines?” and Copilot will provide answers from the transcript.
